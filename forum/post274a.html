The Finobe forums is one of the reasons the majority of the player base is known on the website, it is in many cases a part of Finobe's history and recently players have gone wild archiving as many known posts as they can before Finobe's shutdow- we still have 2 months god damn it-

Anyways, we are gonna talk about the importance of the Finobe forums, mostly talk about users that gained respect by posting here. But let's begin over the historic parts of the forums.

We all know the 1st ever post was made by Raymonf, the founder of Finobe, it was posted on May 19th, 2017 as a welcoming post. Most of the early threads memed it "Forum 2.0" as seen in the 2nd thread, posted by Niall, however most of the early content on the forums is now deleted and lost to time so yeah (OOF) but we can still get over some conclusions. The first ever post was an Announcement, the 2nd was made in General and the 9th was the first Off Topic post on Finobe, which was posted by a player named nintend0wn, all it said was "Oh, Forum 2.0. Nice.".

And then it expanded from there. Now keep in mind post count didn't exist back then on the forums, preventing most players from post farming.

In late 2019 and early 2020 is where the fun really begins, many of the posts are entertaining to read, mostly the 73k posts is the golden age of the forums, but not only the forums, people like Skampus and NINGASAMURI were all over the site and making Finobe's culture to what it is today. Back in that time a lot of now terminated accounts were raiding the forums and the quality of them wasn't so nice back then. Steadily over the months however with the introduction of invite keys it blocked many players from joining, which seems bad for the game's reputation, but was a smart move from getting most of the bad apples away. Also the chances of drama were higher back then.

Now, about the community, nowadays you can recognize most of the players on the forums, people like Facbook and pizzaboxer weren't seen on the forums as much in early 2020. As I said, some of them gained their trust by being active, the famous LMaD'er Frenemy, iceman, pact, Yugoslavia, AccountBackup, and a lot more make a lot of threads that keep the players interested. The Finobe forum community has a lot of down to earth people but most of the OT threads are like 1 line of reading, but whatever. It is easier to get to know people in today's Finobe and they all follow the forum rules as agreed.

Conclusion

So I think that being part of it is a good thing since it helps communicating with the rest of Finobe users and discussing topics together, it's a fun experience in my opinion. Although it has it's not-well-respected history with raids and dramas, at least in todays state it has improved a lot.

Thank you for reading all the way through the end, sorry that I didn't cover most of the incidents that happened, it was a wild ride. Peace!
